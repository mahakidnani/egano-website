Fabrication

Engineered to Perform. Built to Last.

Our fabrication division delivers precision-driven solutions for industrial clients who demand accuracy and durability. We specialise in:

Structural Fabrication

Custom mild steel (MS) and stainless steel (SS) structures designed and manufactured to project requirements.

Piping Works

Complete piping systems including IBR compliant piping for high-pressure and high-temperature industrial applications.

Custom Steel Components

Bespoke components fabricated to meet the specific operational needs of each client, from individual parts to full assemblies.

Equipment Erection

Installation and alignment of heavy industrial equipment including pressure vessels, heat exchangers, and rotating machinery.

Every fabrication job we undertake is approached with a focus on dimensional accuracy, material quality, and compliance with applicable standards. Our team brings the technical skill and field experience to handle complex scopes across industries including beverages, cement, petroleum, rubber, agro-processing, and construction materials.

Manpower Supply

The Right People. Ready When You Need Them.

Industrial projects move on people. Our manpower supply service connects businesses with reliable, job-ready workers both skilled and unskilled across a wide range of industrial sectors.

We provide:

Skilled Tradespeople

Welders, fitters, riggers, pipefitters, structural erectors, and other specialist tradespeople with verified field experience.

Semi-Skilled and General Labour

Dependable workers for support roles, site operations, material handling, and general project tasks.

Flexible Engagement

Whether your requirement is a short-term shutdown crew or a long-term project workforce, we structure our supply to match your timeline and budget.

Workforce Compliance

Teams mobilised with attention to regulatory, safety, and operational requirements for domestic and international projects.

Our manpower solutions have supported projects in India and internationally, giving us a clear understanding of how to mobilise teams efficiently and manage workforce requirements across different regulatory and operational environments.

Sourced Right. Delivered on Specification.
